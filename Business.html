Business

Octobers Very Own

Drake has his own website called "Octobers Very Own" which is what OVO stands for. It shows off all available merch that is for sale along with actual stores that you can go and visit.

Virginia Black

Virginia Black is a whiskey that is a collaboration between Brent Hocking an American entrepreneur and Drake. It was introduced in 2016. It's available in the United States, Canada, U.K., Ghana, Kenya, Rwanda, Tanzania, Zanzibar, Burundi, Uganda, and the United Arab Emirates. "Virginia Black"

Toronto Raptors

On September 30, 2013, at a press conference with Maple Leaf Sports and Entertainment CEO Tim Leiweke, Drake was announced as the new "global ambassador" for the Toronto Raptors, therefore he joined the team's executive team. He helps to promote and host activities. He began hosting a yearly drake night for the Raptors in 2013. Since then he has begun to rebrand the team as far as clothing and gear. The team now has 2 jerseys designed with Drake's inspirations behind them. The jerseys have Drake's known color palette being black, gold, and white. Along with that, the team has certain giveaways for their games that give away Raptors and Drake collaboration items to a certain number of first people to arrive at the stadium.
